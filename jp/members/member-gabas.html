Antonio Gabas
過去のメンバー

経歴

Antonio Gabas received the MSc degree from Universitat Politecnica de Catalunya (UPC), Spain (2013). He is working in the CNRS-AIST Joint Robotics Laboratory (JRL), UMI3218/CRT, Tsukuba, Japan, as PhD student of University of Tsukuba, Tsukuba, Japan, and as a research assistant in JRL (2021-). His research interests include 3D pose estimation, machine learning and reinforcement learning for robotics applications.
