主務研究員 — 個人ウェブサイト

経歴

室岡 雅樹 received the BE, MS, and PhD degree in information science and technology from The University of Tokyo, Japan, in 2013, 2015, and 2018, respectively. He was a project assistant professor in the Department of Mechano-Informatics at The University of Tokyo from 2018 to 2020. He joined the CNRS-AIST Joint Robotics Laboratory in the National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST) in 2020 as a researcher. His research interest includes the motion planning and control of humanoid robots.
