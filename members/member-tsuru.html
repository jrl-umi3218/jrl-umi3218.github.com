Masato Tsuru
Alumni

Bio

Masato Tsuru received the master's degree in engineering from Osaka University, Japan, in 2021. He started working as Research Assistant at the CNRS-AIST JRL (Joint Robotics Laboratory), IRL, AIST in 2019. His research topic is combining humanoid robots with vision sensing and real-time action planning for automonous humanoid robots.
