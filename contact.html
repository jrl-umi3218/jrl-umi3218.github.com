Access

The JRL is located in the AIST Tsukuba Headquarters and Information Technology Collaborative Research Center, AIST Tsukuba Central 1. You can find general instructions to reach AIST Tsukuba Central 1 in this page. This page provides supplementary information.

  • From Narita International Airport

    The easiest way to come from Narita International Airport to Tsukuba is to take a direct bus. Instructions for coming to Tsukuba by bus are given in this page but the time table in the page is outdated. From Dec. 5, 2022, there are five buses per day from Narita to Tsukuba.

    If you cannot take a bus, you need to take trains to reach the Tsukuba station.

  • From Haneda Airport

    The easiest way to come from Haneda Airport to Tsukuba is to take a direct bus. Instructions are given in this page but the time table in the page is outdated. From 29 April 2022, there are four buses per day from Haneda to Tsukuba. The buses depart Terminal 3 at 9:20, 12:15, 17:05 and 19:05. These buses arrive at Terminal 2 ten minutes after departing from Terminal 3, and another five minutes later at Terminal 1. The bus stop Namiki 2-chome is in front of AIST Tsukuba Central.

    If you cannot take a bus, you need to take trains to reach the Tsukuba station.

  • From Tsukuba Center Bus Terminal / Tsukuba Station

    From Tsukuba Center Bus Terminal, you can take public bus, taxi, or AIST shuttle bus. Instructions are given in this page.
